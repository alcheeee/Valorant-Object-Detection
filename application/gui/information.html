Valorant Object Detection
•Width and Height•
If no sizing is put into the Width or Height box, the resolution of your original image or video will be used.
Recommended to 640x640 for most accurate results.
•Frame-rate and Confidence•
The frame-rate will be how the video is sent to the model, so the model will render with that frame-rate.
Recommended to leave default.
•How to Upload•
Click the ‘Upload Folder’ button and put any image or video in there.
•Supported Filetypes•
Images: ['.bmp', '.dng', '.jpeg', '.jpg', '.mpo', '.png', '.tif', '.tiff', '.webp', '.pfm']
Videos: ['.asf', '.avi', '.gif', '.m4v', '.mkv', '.mov', '.mp4', '.mpeg', '.mpg', '.ts', '.wmv', '.webm']
